In 2018 I ended up in the hospital for taking a massive dose of LSD and mushrooms. Someone sold me about 10 times the dose I was expecting, and I spiraled into something. Psychosis, derealization, whatever you want to call it.

I was falling through space, cold, alone, with nothing to catch me. All my defenses disappeared and for years after, I had no confidence, sure that I had gone insane and ruined my future.

Some part of me was still there though, and I fought hard.

I started making psychedelic artworks inspired by the pure pain and lack of identity I was feeling, and slowly my brain started to get well again.

I found my inner power, and it was even greater than I could imagine.
